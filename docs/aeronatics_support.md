# AeroNatics Support

[← Back to App Launchpad]({{ '/' | relative_url }})

We are here to help you get the most out of AeroNatics. Use the options below to request assistance, report issues, or share feedback.

## Contact the team

- **Email:** [moocbear@gmail.com](mailto:moocbear@gmail.com)
- **Support hours:** Monday–Friday, 9:00–17:00 EST
- **Response target:** within 2 business days

When you reach out, please include:

1. AeroNatics app version (Settings → bottom of page).
2. Device model and OS version.
3. A clear description of the issue, reproduction steps, and screenshots or screen recordings if available.

## Common topics

- **Account / subscription changes:** Email us with the email or nickname you signed up with.
- **Connectivity or data mismatches:** Note which feature is affected  
- **Feature suggestions:** Tell us how the enhancement would improve your workflow so we can prioritize it.

## Service status

All AeroNatics services are operating normally. We will post maintenance notices and outage updates here if that changes.

## Escalation

Add “URGENT” to your subject line for safety-related issues. Those messages are monitored closely, and we will respond as quickly as possible.
