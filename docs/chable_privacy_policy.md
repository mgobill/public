# Chable Privacy Policy

[← Back to App Launchpad]({{ '/' | relative_url }})

_Last updated: 2026-04-13_

This Privacy Policy explains how Chable collects, uses, and shares information when you use the app.

## 1. Information we collect

### Account information
- **Email address and authentication credentials** used to create and access your account (handled via Firebase Authentication).

### Gameplay and profile data
- **Company profile details** such as company name, motto, and optional logo.
- **Portfolio records** including acquired stores, chain names, purchase cost, debt terms, and timestamps.
- **Bank and economy records** including cash balance, transaction history, and starting-cash settings derived from app configuration.

### Location data
- **Current location** is used when running scans for nearby businesses.
- Chable uses this location to retrieve nearby place data from OpenStreetMap/Overpass services.
- We do not store continuous location tracking history.

### Admin and content data
- **Official and queued business records** (names, categories, financial templates, and metadata) managed through the admin console.
- **Optional CSV import/export files** processed by admins for queue updates.

### Technical and diagnostic data
- Basic logs and error details may be used to diagnose crashes, failed scans, and service reliability issues.

## 2. How we use your information
- Provide core gameplay features (scanning, matching, acquisitions, portfolio management).
- Synchronize your data across signed-in devices.
- Maintain official business datasets and admin moderation workflows.
- Improve reliability, performance, and support response quality.

## 3. Information sharing
- We do not sell your personal data.
- We use service providers needed to run the app, including Firebase services and third-party map/place APIs.
- Data may be shared when required by law or to protect the app, users, or infrastructure.

## 4. Where data is stored
- **Firebase Authentication** for account access.
- **Cloud Firestore** for gameplay state, queue data, and configuration.
- **Firebase Storage** for user-uploaded media (for example, company logos), when applicable.

## 5. Your choices
- Control location permissions through device settings.
- Update or remove editable profile data inside the app.
- Contact support to request help with account or data concerns.

## 6. Data retention
- We keep data while your account remains active and as needed to provide the service.
- Data may remain in routine backups for a limited period after deletion requests.

## 7. Children
Chable is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region).

## 8. Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised date.
