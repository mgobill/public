# Chable Support

[← Back to App Launchpad]({{ '/' | relative_url }})

Thanks for using Chable. This page covers support, bug reports, and feature requests.

## Contact the team

- **Email:** [moocbear@gmail.com](mailto:moocbear@gmail.com)

When you reach out, please include:

1. Your Chable app version and device model.
2. OS version and your current scan radius setting.
3. A clear description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and screenshots when possible.

## Common topics

- **Scanning errors:** Include your scan radius, whether location permissions are enabled, and the exact error text.
- **Business matching questions:** Share the business name, category, and whether it was expected to match a primary listing or type fallback.
- **Portfolio or cash balance issues:** Include approximate timestamps, actions taken, and the business names involved.
- **Admin queue import/export:** Attach the CSV file (if applicable) and note which rows failed.

## Service status

No current outages are reported. This page will be updated if planned maintenance impacts Chable services.

## Escalation

For urgent issues that block app use, include "URGENT" in the subject line.
