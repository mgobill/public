# ORAD DDx Privacy Policy

[‚Üê Back to App Launchpad]({{ '/' | relative_url }})

_Last updated: 2025-12-28_

ORAD DDx is an offline reference tool for oral radiology differentials. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

## 1. Data we collect

ORAD DDx does not require an account and does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## 2. Device analytics

ORAD DDx does not run third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. If you choose to share crash or diagnostics data with Apple, that data is handled by Apple under their policies.

## 3. Data sharing

We do not sell or share personal data because we do not collect it.

## 4. Data retention

All usage stays on your device. There is no server-side storage for ORAD DDx.

## 5. Children

ORAD DDx is not directed to children under 13 (or under the minimum age in your region).

## 6. Changes

If our practices change, we will update this policy and revise the date above.

Questions or requests: denllz@nus.edu.sg
