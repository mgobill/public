# ORAD DDx Support

[← Back to App Launchpad]({{ '/' | relative_url }})

Thanks for using ORAD DDx. This page is the fastest way to request help, report issues, or suggest improvements.

## Contact the team

- **Email:** [moocbear@gmail.com](mailto:moocbear@gmail.com)
- **Support hours:** Monday–Friday, 9:00–17:00 EST
- **Response target:** within 2 business days

When you reach out, please include:

1. Your ORAD DDx app version (from TestFlight/App Store build details).
2. Device model and iOS version.
3. A short description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and screenshots if available.

## Common topics

- **Filtering questions:** Share which factors you selected and what results you expected.
- **Data issues:** Point to the specific diagnosis and factor combination that looks incorrect.
- **Feature requests:** Tell us how the change would improve your workflow.

## Service status

ORAD DDx works fully offline and does not rely on servers. If anything changes, updates will be noted here.

## Escalation

If something is urgent, add “URGENT” to the email subject line. We monitor that inbox closely.
