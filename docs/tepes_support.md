Thanks for using Tepes. This page covers support, bug reports, and feature requests.

## Contact the team

- **Email:** [moocbear@gmail.com](mailto:moocbear@gmail.com)

When you reach out, please include:

1. Your Tepes app version and device model.
2. OS version and whether location services are enabled.
3. A clear description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and screenshots if available.

## Common topics

- **Location issues:** Confirm location permissions are allowed for Tepes and that mock locations are disabled.
- **Account sign-in or verification:** Share the email you used and any error message shown.
- **Posting or photos:** Let us know whether the issue occurs when adding text, photos, or both.
- **Report or delete content:** Tell us the tepe text, approximate time, and why it should be reviewed.

## Service status

No current outages are reported. We will update this page if maintenance impacts Tepes services.

## Escalation

For urgent concerns, include "URGENT" in the email subject line so we can prioritize your request.
